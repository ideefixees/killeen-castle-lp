Hunters Yard Lodges

The Hunters Yard lodges are located beside the Walled Garden and overlook the Jack Nicklaus signature course. The guest bedrooms are complete with superking sized double/twin bed with the finest linen, a smart TV & high-speed internet. Bathrooms feature walk-in rain head showers, some with a separate bath & a mist free mirror. Please note, the photo is representative of the room category. Actual rooms may vary from what is shown.

Rose Cottage

The Rose Cottage is located beside the 11th Century Castle, and overlooks the Jack Nicklaus signature course. The cottage has two superior styles spacious bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms, free standing bath in one and rain head showers in both. Please note, the photo is representative of the room category.
